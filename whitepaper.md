# ALBX Whitepaper  
## Albexc Ecosystem Token


## 1. Introduction

ALBX is the native utility token of the Albexc platform, designed to power a decentralized ecosystem focused on prediction markets, user incentives, and digital interactions.

Built on the Polygon network, ALBX leverages fast transactions and low fees to enable scalable and efficient user experiences.


## 2. Vision

The goal of Albexc is to create a user-friendly, transparent, and scalable ecosystem where users can:

- Participate in prediction markets  
- Earn rewards through engagement  
- Use ALBX as a native digital currency  
- Access platform features and services  

ALBX acts as the core economic layer of this system.


## 3. Token Overview

- Token Name: Albexc  
- Symbol: ALBX  
- Standard: ERC-20  
- Network: Polygon  
- Total Supply: 100,000,000  
- Decimals: 18  

ALBX is a fixed supply token with no inflation or minting functionality.


## 4. Token Utility

ALBX is designed to serve multiple functions within the Albexc platform:

### 4.1 Payments
Used for transactions within the platform ecosystem.

### 4.2 Prediction Markets
Users can stake ALBX on outcomes of events.

### 4.3 Rewards
Users earn ALBX through participation and engagement.

### 4.4 Fees
ALBX is used to pay platform fees and services.


## 5. Tokenomics

The total supply of ALBX is fixed at 100,000,000 tokens.
